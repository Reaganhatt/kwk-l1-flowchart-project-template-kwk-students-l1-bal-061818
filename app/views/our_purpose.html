Our Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people with food allergies find what they can eat at restaurants near them, decreasing the risk of an individual having an allergic reaction to a food by making them aware if their allergen is in a food before they choose the restaurant. Our goal is to make the world a safer and more enjoyable place for people with allergies and intolerances.

Here are the top 8 most common allergies:

  1. Eggs

  2. Milk

  3. Tree nuts

  4. Soy

  5. Peanuts

  6. Fish

  7. Shellfish

  8. Gluten
